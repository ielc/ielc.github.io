Do

Using the Qur’an Halaqah lesson plans, lead a series of halaqah with your learners. Remind the learners of the Halaqah Ground Rules you devised together last week. Give the learners time to work through the Key Questions. Remember that the educator’s role is to facilitate the learners to come to their own answer together. You can help them towards the end of the dialogue by confirming, clarifying and expanding on their answers. Support the learners engage in cumulative dialogue by periodically asking them if this discussion reminds them of anything they have learnt before. How does it relate to their prior learning.
