Discuss

  • In the Week 1 discussion page, post a response to at least one of these questions:
    • What has been lost in confining our thinking about education to modern schooling?
    • What is education? What is its purpose?
    • In what ways might we understand al-Baqarah: 29-38 and al-Alaq: 1-5 as dialogue?
    • Thinking from within an Islamic paradigm, is education inextricably linked to dialogue?
  • Now, read through others’ responses to these questions and make sure you post at least one reply with your response to at least one person’s ideas.
  • Remember that the discussion forum is your ‘space’ to reflect with others so feel free to engage others’ ideas and to post questions for others.
