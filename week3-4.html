Do

  • Using the prompts in the Reflective Journal, think about the unique character and qualities of one person you know, this could be a learner you work with or someone else. Write a ‘Pen Portrait’ of this person. What makes them unique? How do they differ from their siblings? How is it that two people are raised by the same parents but are so different?
  • Discuss the following provocative statement with someone close to you. This could be a member of your family or a colleague: “My relationships make me who I am”. Use this to make further notes in your reflective journal.
